Level: Senior #ARC-082

Explain the Trade-offs between Microservices and Monoliths in High-Concurrency Systems.

A strong answer should cover:

  • Latency: Monoliths have lower internal latency (in-memory calls) vs Microservices (network overhead).
  • Scalability: Microservices allow selective scaling of bottlenecks.
  • Complexity: Distributed transaction management (Saga pattern) vs ACID in monoliths.
